Origin of the Margarita

The origins of any cocktail can be slighlty ambigous, which also is true when it comes to the margarita. One of the strongest claims of inventing the margarita was made by Francisco "Pancho" Morales, in 1942. He worked in a bar named Tommy's Place in Ciudad Juarez in Mexico and allegedly invented the cocktail when a guest came in asking for a "Magnolia".
